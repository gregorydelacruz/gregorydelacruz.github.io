Background

Builder. Publisher.
Perpetual Operator.

I started in enterprise networking at Nortel Networks — a career that taught me systems thinking, the cost of failure at scale, and the discipline of testing before deploying. I've carried those habits into everything I've built since.

For 22+ years I've operated in the WordPress ecosystem — not as a developer for hire, but as a builder of my own properties. I understand hosting, performance, SEO, content pipelines, affiliate infrastructure, and the economics of niche publishing from the inside out.

Today I run a pickleball content network covering courts, gear, community, and culture — built with WordPress, automated with Make.com and the Anthropic API, and optimized for both readers and search. I also publish independently on Amazon across multiple niche categories.

The common thread across everything I build: systems that produce value without requiring constant manual intervention. Content that earns. Sites that scale. Operations that run.
